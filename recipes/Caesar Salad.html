Caesar Salad

Description

Leafy greens with protein, croutons, and a savory sauce

Ingredients

  • leafy greens
  • croutons
  • protein of choice
  • caesar dressing

Instructions

  1. First, chop and add leafy greens to a bowl
  2. Second, chop and add your choice of protein.
  3. Third, add croutons
  4. Finally, add dressing and toss
