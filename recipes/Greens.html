Greens

Description

Southern greens fried down in delicious bacon grease with cajun seasoning

Ingredients

  • Collard Greens
  • Bacon
  • Tony Chachares or other cajun seasoning

Instructions

  1. Slice bacon into small pieces.
  2. Fry bacon down in large pan
  3. Add greens and season to taste
  4. Fry until cooked down.
